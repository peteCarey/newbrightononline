Log On

To log into your account you will need your USERNAME and PASSWORD handy. Your user name will be your first name, followed by a full stop, then your surname.

EXAMPLE USERNAME: JOE.BLOGGS

The default password is set to changeme. If you are unsure of your password please ask the administrator for assistance. We advise you keep your password safe and change it from the default one as soon as possible.

Password hints. Pick a password you will remember. We advise you make it at least 9 characters long for security purposes.

Send Email

To send and receive email on your New Brighton Online email account you need to follow these steps.

Step 1 - Double click the ‘Outlook Express’ icon on the desktop. This will your email client.

Step 2 – When the client has loaded it will ask you for your password. Enter your password into the box and click OK. Please note if you do not enter your password and you start typing a email you will loose all the work you have done. You must enter your password first before doing anything else.

Step 3 – After entering your password the system will automatically check your email and inform you if you have any new messages.

Step 4 – To create a new email, click the NEW MESSAGE icon. This will open a blank email. You will then need to do three things. Enter a recipient (who you would like to send the email too). Enter a subject title. Create a message.

Step 5 – To send your new email all you have to do is press the SEND MESSAGE icon and your email will have been sent.
