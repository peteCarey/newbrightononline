Where Are We

New Brighton Online is located at the New Brighton Community Centre Hope Street.We are situated just off Rowson Street. Our location is very central for public transport and we have parking facilities outside the community centre on Hope Street. For information on public transport time tables please visit www.merseytravel.gov.uk
