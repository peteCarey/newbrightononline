Cinderella

Saturday 10th December 2016 to 08th January 2016

Various times

With hit songs, a live band, a stunning bespoke set, fabulous costumes, the talented cast includes: This Morning's Alison Hammond as the "Fairy Godmother". The welcome return of Olivia Birchenough from Channel 5's Milkshake! as "Cinderella". Comedian Tam Ryan as "Buttons" and "Ugly Sisters" Jamie Morris and Tarot Joseph.

Christmas with the Wirral Symphony Orchestra

Sunday 11/12/2016

2.15pm and 5.30pm

Wirral Symphony Orchestra returns with two Yuletide concerts, with music for all ages to get your Christmas well and truly underway. Wirral Symphony Orchestra will be joined again by the children's choirs from Christ the King Primary School, Bromborough and St Peter's Primary School, Heswall. There will be a selection of seasonal readings.

Christmas Party Night - Abba Forever

Tuesday 13th December 2016

7:00pm

Established in 1998, the show has been recognised as one of the best recreations of Abba live in concert and performs at many of the U.K's most prestigious theatres up and down the country. At present, they are on a national U.K. tour, endeavouring to get theatre audiences on their feet and enjoying the music of Abba.
