Please donate and help keep New Brighton Community Centre Open

The Centre is now 34 years old and over that time generations of residents and families have enjoyed the services, activities, information and support we have provided.

Unfortunately we are struggling to keep the centre open, as a result we have created a donation page and would be grateful for any help you can give. By clicking the Donate button below your donation will ensure that future generations can continue to contribute and enjoy all that the Community Centre has to offer.

So much done, and so much more to do, help us continue to support the New Brighton Community.

Many thanks from everyone at New Brighton Community Centre!
